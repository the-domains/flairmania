ABOUT FLAIR MANIA

OlyBet FlairMania is a competition of World’s best bartenders held in Olympic Voodoo Casino, Riga, Latvia.

Riga becomes the flair capital of the World each January since 2011. During four years about 90 world’s best bartenders from Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia, Finland, Belarus, Turkey, Czech Republic, Russia, Germany, Austria, Ukraine, Poland, Italy, Romania, Bulgaria, France, Spain, Turkey, Armenia, Uzbekistan, Japan, Argentina, Switzerland and UK participated with fantastic performances. The event is a part of World Flair Association’s Grand Slam, enabling participants to earn rating points in World’s most prestigious flair bartenders ranking.
